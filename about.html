About helpMate

helpMate is a dedicated platform designed to support individuals in understanding and managing their mental health. Our mission is to make mental health assessment accessible, confidential, and user-friendly.

Using advanced machine learning algorithms and expert-designed questionnaires, we help identify potential signs of depression and provide personalized insights to support your mental well-being journey.

Our team of mental health professionals and technology experts work together to ensure that our assessment tools are both accurate and easy to use, making mental health support more accessible to everyone.
